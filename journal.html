Journal Club

The Club

The McGill Physiology Student Journal Club is an organization designed for Physiology students, by Physiology students, in order to better our education in clinical and basic biomedical research journal comprehension. This club hopes to foster a deeper understanding of how to read, present, and analyze scientific literature - skills essential to becoming excellent members of the scientific community. In addition, we aim to collaborate in order to write and publish a yearly review article.

Please join our Facebook Group to learn more about how you can get involved!

About us

The Physiology Undergraduate League of Students (PULS) is the elected student government of the Department of Physiology at McGill University in Montreal, Quebec, Canada.

Views expressed on this site are not necessarily those of McGill University, or McGill University's Department of Physiology.
